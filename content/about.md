## Bio

Born and still living in Dublin. Irish and Dutch nationalities.

I'm very active and outgoing.


## Research Interests

I have recently graduated from UCD with first class honours, BSc. Physics with Astronomy and Space Science. I’m particularly interested in computing, and its applications to the field.

From September, I am starting a PhD with the Planetary Magnetospheres group at the Dublin Institute for Advanced Studies. I will be studying Mercury’s magnetosphere and its interaction with the solar environment through analysis of large data sets from the MESSENGER mission, and upon its arrival, BepiColombo.

## Hobbies

Outside of my research, I do lots of other hobbies! I love the outdoors, I'm an avid hiker and enjoy camping. I also do whitewater and sea kayaking.

I have a huge interest in music, and play the piano and electric guitar!
