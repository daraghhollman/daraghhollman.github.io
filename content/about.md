## Research Interests

My research interests are... maths and programming in space science.

From September, I am starting a PhD with the Planetary Magnetospheres group at the Dublin Institute for Advanced Studies. I will be studying Mercury's magnetosphere and its interaction with the solar environment through analysis of large data sets from the MESSENGER mission, and upon its arrival, BepiColombo.

I'm a Government of Ireland Postgraduate Scholar, fully funded by the Irish Research Council. I have recently graduated from UCD with first class honours, BSc. Physics with Astonomy and Space Science.
