In early 2025, I was successful in receiving a [France Excellence Research Residency](https://ie.diplomatie.gouv.fr/en/relations-bilaterales/cultural-educational-and-scientific-cooperation/2026-france-excellence), funding a week of collaboration between two research organisations in Paris (6th-10th October 2025). My accommodation, travel, and subsistence were fully covered by the residency grant.


This included discussions primarily with Lina Z. Hadid, Sae Aizawa, Nicolas Aunai ([LPP](https://www.lpp.polytechnique.fr/?lang=fr)), Léa Griton ([LESIA](https://lesia.obspm.fr/)), and their students and colleagues. At both of these institutes, I gave a joint seminar with Caitríona M. Jackman where we discussed 'how to creatively account for a lack of an upstream monitor' at planets other than Earth.\
